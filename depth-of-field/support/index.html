Common questions

What circle-of-confusion does this use?

Standard values: 0.030mm (Full Frame), 0.020mm (APS-C), 0.015mm (Micro 4/3). These match what most DOF references and printed lens manuals use for ~8×10" prints viewed at 25cm.

Why does my real-world DOF look different?

DOF is partly a perception thing. Larger prints, closer viewing, cropped output, and individual eyesight all shift what looks "in focus." This app gives the standard mathematical answer — use it as a planning baseline.
